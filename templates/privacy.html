Privacy & Policy

Your privacy and trust matter to us.

Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information on Proest Learning.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information to provide and improve our learning platform:

  • Account Information: Username and password when you create an account
  • Usage Data: Course progress, quiz scores, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address
  • Analytics: Page visits, time spent, and features used

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track course progress and achievement milestones
  • Improve platform features and course quality
  • Provide technical support and security
  • Send occasional updates about new courses or features

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password storage with hashing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data only:

  • With legal authorities when required by law
  • With service providers who assist in platform operations (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for login and session management
  • Analytics Cookies: Track usage patterns to improve features
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices

You can control cookie settings in your browser preferences.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period after account deletion to support backups and legal compliance. You can request data deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data anytime
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of promotional emails
  • Export your course progress and achievements

8. Contact Us

If you have concerns about your privacy or wish to exercise any of your rights, please reach out to us:

Email: privacy@learnhub.example

Support: hello@learnhub.example

9. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on the platform. Your continued use of Proest Learning after updates indicates your acceptance of the new terms.

10. Children's Privacy

Proest Learning is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the data promptly and notify parents or guardians.
