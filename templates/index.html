Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information to provide and improve our learning platform:

  • Account Information: Username and password when you create an account
  • Usage Data: Course progress, quiz scores, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address
  • Analytics: Page visits, time spent, and features used

How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track course progress and achievement milestones
  • Improve platform features and course quality
  • Provide technical support and security
  • Send occasional updates about new courses or features

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for login and session management
  • Analytics Cookies: Track usage patterns to improve features
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices

You can control cookie settings in your browser preferences.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data only:

  • With legal authorities when required by law
  • With service providers who assist in platform operations (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password storage with hashing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data
